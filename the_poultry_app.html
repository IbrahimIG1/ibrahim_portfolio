Description:-

The Poultry is a smart social media platform designed specifically for the poultry industry, connecting engineers, veterinarians, farmers, and home breeders in one ecosystem. The app offers a variety of innovative features:

AI-Powered Image Diagnosis: Users can capture/upload chicken images via the camera to diagnose diseases automatically.
Smart Chatbot: A smart assistant dedicated to answering poultry-related queries.
Cycle Management Dashboard: Generates detailed reports on the user's poultry cycles, comparing real-time data to ideal benchmarks.
Market Prices Section: Stay updated with live poultry product prices.
Educational Videos: Access a library of professional training videos and tutorials.
Medical Search Engine: Easily search for diseases, treatments, active ingredients, vaccines, and medications.
Multilingual Support: Fully available in Arabic and English.
